OUR MISSION


We stand for empowering the youth of Chicago. Our mission is to ensure children have opportunities outside of the classroom to thrive and succeed. We collaborate with initiatives and charities who provide after school programing and extra-curricular activities to better the lives of children and families across Chicago.


Results

After school programming increases graduation rates for children

Activities

We provide programming to take burdens off parents and help students learn

Impact

Every dollar get another student closer to accessing these programs


OUR PROCESS

Community Currency is a registered 501(c)(3) organization that salvages leftover foreign currency from international travel, re-purposing it to fund local, community-oriented charities. After collecting the currency via receptacles in airports across the country, a third party converts our proceeds, and we distribute them to our deserving partners.


1. Landing

Travelers land in international airports, returning with foreign currency

2. Placement

They conveniently place leftover money in our donation bins

3. Collection

100% of collected funds go to after-school programming for children in need
